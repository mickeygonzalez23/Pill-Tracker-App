# Privacy Policy for Pill Tracker Voice Log

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Pill Tracker Voice Log is designed for personal medication tracking.

The app stores medication names, private medication nicknames, dose schedules, reminder settings, and dose history on your device. This information is used only to provide the app's tracking, reminder, notification, and voice command features.

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or third parties. The app does not use analytics, advertising networks, account sign-in, or external servers.

Notifications and voice command features are handled by Apple's iOS system services on your device. Your use of those features may be subject to Apple's privacy practices and device settings.

This app is for personal medication tracking only. It does not provide medical advice and is not a substitute for guidance from a healthcare professional.

For support, contact the developer through the support link listed on the App Store product page.
